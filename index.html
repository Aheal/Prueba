Bienvenido a Marina Barracuda

This is the place where we are sure you would live an unforgettable experience!

Here at Marina Barracuda we had more than 20 years of experience. Our great location and comfortable facilities offer you the best place to relax before the adventure begins. Marina Barracuda offer water activities like The Mangrove Tour and The Jungle Tour, where the tourists had the chance to drive his own boat to visit amazing natural places and get to know the mangrove vegetation in the zone, experiencing also the adrenaline of the speed across the lagoon Nichupté. Definitively a must to do if you’re visiting Cancun.
